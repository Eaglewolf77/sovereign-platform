
# Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

This directory contains Architecture Decision Records (ADR) for the Sovereign Platform.

ADRs document important architectural decisions, including context, reasoning, and trade-offs.

---

## 🧠 Purpose

The goal of ADRs is to:

- Capture why decisions were made
    
- Provide historical context for architectural choices
    
- Document trade-offs and alternatives
    
- Make the platform easier to understand and evolve
    

---

## 📄 Structure

Each ADR follows a simple structure:

- **Title** – What the decision is about
    
- **Status** – Proposed / Accepted / Deprecated
    
- **Context** – Problem or situation
    
- **Decision** – What was chosen
    
- **Consequences** – Trade-offs and impact

## 🔄 Lifecycle

ADRs are living documents and may evolve as the platform changes.

New ADRs are added when significant architectural decisions are made.

---

## 🎯 Why ADR?

This platform is designed with a strong focus on:

- Identity-first architecture
    
- Zero Trust principles
    
- Sovereignty and self-hosting
    

ADRs ensure that these principles are consistently applied and understood.
